      Security & Privacy Policy

      Your trust and safety are our highest priority

      🛡️ 100% Secure Payments

      All payments on Devangi Products are processed through a certified, RBI-licensed payment gateway. Your financial information is fully encrypted and never stored on our servers.

      🔐 256-bit SSL Encryption ✅ PCI DSS Compliant 🏦 RBI Licensed Gateway 🔄 3D Secure Authentication
      📋

      Information We Collect

      We collect information necessary to process your orders and provide a better shopping experience:

      • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and shipping address
      • Account Information: Username and encrypted password (we never store plain-text passwords)
      • Order Information: Products purchased, order history, and delivery preferences
      • Device Information: Browser type, IP address (for security and analytics purposes)
      💳

      Payment Security

      We take payment security very seriously. Here's how your payment data is protected:

      • All transactions are processed by our certified payment partner — we never see or store your card details
      • Payments are secured with 256-bit AES encryption to protect all data in transit
      • Our checkout is PCI DSS Level 1 compliant — the highest security standard for card processing
      • 3D Secure (OTP verification) is required for added transaction safety
      • All payment pages are served over HTTPS with a valid SSL certificate
      🔍

      How We Use Your Information

      Your data is used solely to serve you better. We use your information to:

      • Process and fulfill your orders
      • Send order confirmation and shipment tracking emails
      • Provide customer support
      • Improve our website and product offerings
      • Send promotional offers (only if you opt-in)

      We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

      🛡️

      How We Protect Your Data

      • All data transmitted between your browser and our server is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
      • Passwords are stored using strong bcrypt hashing — never in plain text
      • Access to customer data is restricted to authorized personnel only
      • Regular security audits and vulnerability testing are performed
      • Our servers are protected by firewalls and intrusion detection systems
      🍪

      Cookies Policy

      We use cookies to enhance your shopping experience:

      • Essential Cookies: Keep you logged in and remember your cart items
      • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site (anonymous data only)
      • Preference Cookies: Remember your language and display preferences

      You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect site functionality.
