      Our Story

      Devangi Products was founded with a simple dream — to provide every tailor, designer, and sewing enthusiast with the finest quality sewing materials at affordable prices.

      Located in the heart of Surat, Gujarat — India's textile capital — we have been serving thousands of customers with premium threads, needles, fabrics, and accessories for over a decade.

      We believe that great craftsmanship starts with great tools. That's why we carefully curate every product in our collection to meet the highest standards of quality and durability.
